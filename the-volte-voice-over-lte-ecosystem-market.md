<p><ul><li>CSFB</li><li>VOIMS</li><li>Dual Radio/SVLTE</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The VoLTE ecosystem encompasses various market types for transmitting voice over LTE networks. CSFB (Circuit-Switched FallBack) allows voice calls to switch to 2G/3G networks when necessary, while VoIMS (Voice over IP Multimedia Subsystem) utilizes IP networks for voice calls, providing advanced features and seamless integration with data services. Dual Radio/SVLTE (Simultaneous Voice and LTE) enables simultaneous voice and data transmission over LTE, enhancing user experience without compromising call quality. Each type addresses different network capabilities and user needs in voice communication.</p></p>
