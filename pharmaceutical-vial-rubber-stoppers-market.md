<p><p>The pharmaceutical vial rubber stoppers market is divided into two main types: conventional stoppers and film-coated stoppers. Conventional stoppers are made from elastic rubber materials, providing effective sealing and preventing contamination. They are widely used for various pharmaceutical applications. In contrast, film-coated stoppers have an additional layer that enhances their barrier properties, offering improved protection against moisture and gases. This coating helps maintain the stability of sensitive formulations, making them ideal for parenteral and biotech products.</p></p>
