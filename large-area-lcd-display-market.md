<p><ul><li>Static</li><li>Simple Matrix</li><li>Active Matrix</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The large area LCD display market comprises three main types: static, simple matrix, and active matrix displays. Static displays feature fixed images, ideal for signage and low-cost applications. Simple matrix displays utilize a grid of pixels to show basic graphics and text, suitable for simpler tasks. Active matrix displays, the most advanced, use individual transistors for each pixel, providing higher resolution and better performance, making them perfect for high-quality video and graphics applications in modern devices like televisions and monitors.</p></p>
