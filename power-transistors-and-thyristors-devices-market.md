<p><p>The power transistors and thyristors market comprises various semiconductor devices used for controlling and switching electrical power. Power MOSFETs are favored for their efficiency in low-voltage applications, while IGBTs (Insulated Gate Bipolar Transistors) excel in medium to high-power applications due to their high efficiency and fast switching. Bipolar power transistors are used for linear and switching applications, yet have slower switching speeds. Thyristors are robust for high-voltage and high-current control, particularly in industrial and power grid applications.</p></p>
