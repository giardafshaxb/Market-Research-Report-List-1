<p><strong>The Hotel Reservation Software Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Cloud Based</li><li>Premise Based</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Hotel reservation software is typically categorized into two main types: cloud-based and premise-based. Cloud-based solutions are hosted on the vendor's servers, allowing hotels to access the system over the internet, which ensures easy updates, scalability, and remote access. In contrast, premise-based software is installed locally on the hotel's hardware, providing more control and customization but requiring significant upfront investment and ongoing maintenance. Each type caters to different operational needs and budget considerations for hotel management.</p></p>
