<p><p>The tire pyrolysis furnace market is primarily categorized into two types: continuous type and batch type. Continuous pyrolysis furnaces operate continuously, allowing for a steady feed of tire material, which optimizes efficiency and production. In contrast, batch type furnaces process tires in discrete batches, offering flexibility in operational scale and feedstock variety. Both types are utilized for converting waste tires into valuable products like oil, carbon black, and gas, contributing to sustainability and waste management efforts.</p></p>
