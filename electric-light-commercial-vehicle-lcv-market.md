Plug-in Hybrid Electric Vehicles (PHEV) combine a conventional engine with an electric motor, allowing for extended range and flexibility in fuel use. Hybrid Electric Vehicles (HEV) utilize both electric and internal combustion engines, improving fuel efficiency and reducing emissions without needing a charging infrastructure.</p></p>
