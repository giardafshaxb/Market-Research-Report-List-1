<p><p>The surge generator market is divided into two main types: high voltage surge generators and low voltage surge generators. High voltage surge generators are designed for testing the withstand voltage of electrical equipment and measuring insulation performance under extreme conditions, typically exceeding 1000 volts. In contrast, low voltage surge generators operate at lower voltages, usually below 1000 volts, and are primarily used for testing electronic devices and systems against transient overvoltages. Both types are essential for ensuring reliability and safety in electrical applications.</p></p>
