<p><ul><li>Combined Flow</li><li>Parallel Flow</li><li>Counter Flow</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The portable evaporative coolers market includes three primary types: combined flow, parallel flow, and counter flow. Combined flow coolers utilize both cross and downward air movement, enhancing efficiency and cooling capacity. Parallel flow models allow air and water to flow in the same direction, providing a simple design with consistent cooling. Counter flow coolers, on the other hand, have air moving upward while water descends, maximizing contact and evaporation, resulting in effective temperature reduction. Each type serves varied cooling needs and settings.</p></p>
