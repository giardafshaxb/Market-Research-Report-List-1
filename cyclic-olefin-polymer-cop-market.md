The market for COP includes two main types: Cyclic Olefin Copolymer (COC) and pure COP. COC combines different monomers, enhancing properties for applications like packaging and medical devices, while COP is typically used in optical and high-performance applications due to its superior clarity and strength. Both types are integral to advanced materials in various industries.</p></p>
