<p><ul><li>Low Solid</li><li>High Solid</li><li>Solvent-free</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The tar-free epoxy paint market comprises three main types: low solid, high solid, and solvent-free. Low solid epoxy paints contain a higher percentage of solvents, making them easier to apply but less durable. High solid options feature a greater concentration of epoxy resin, resulting in thicker coatings with enhanced durability and lower VOC emissions. Solvent-free epoxy paints eliminate solvents entirely, offering minimal environmental impact and superior safety, while still delivering excellent adhesion and chemical resistance for various applications.</p></p>
