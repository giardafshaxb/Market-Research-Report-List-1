<p><strong>The Planar Diffused Photodiodes Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>PN Type</li><li>PIN Type</li><li>Avalanche Type</li><li>Other</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The planar diffused photodiode market features several key types: </p><p>1. **PN Type**: This basic structure involves p-type and n-type semiconductor layers, offering low sensitivity but simplicity.</p><p>  </p><p>2. **PIN Type**: Enhances performance with an intrinsic layer, providing higher speed and sensitivity, making it suitable for optical communication.</p><p>3. **Avalanche Type**: Operates under reverse bias, enabling internal gain and increased sensitivity, ideal for low-light conditions.</p><p>4. **Other Types**: Includes specialized designs like Schottky photodiodes or hybrid systems, catering to specific applications in sensing and imaging.</p></p>
