<p><p>The Forged Steel Two-Piece Floating Ball Valve market is categorized into Soft Seal and Hard Seal types. Soft Seal valves utilize elastomeric materials to create a tight seal, ensuring excellent leak prevention and low torque operation, making them suitable for low to medium pressure applications. In contrast, Hard Seal valves employ metal-to-metal contact for sealing, offering higher durability and better performance under extreme temperatures and pressures, making them ideal for harsh environments. Both types cater to diverse industrial needs.</p></p>
