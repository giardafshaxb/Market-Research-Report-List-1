<p><p>The Internal Fixation Bone Product market encompasses devices used to stabilize broken bones, primarily through plates, screws, and nails. Plates are metal devices affixed to the bone surface to provide support and alignment. Screws are threaded fasteners that secure bone fragments together, enhancing stability during healing. Nails are long rods inserted into the medullary cavity of long bones, offering robust stabilization. Together, these products facilitate effective surgical interventions, promoting optimal patient recovery and restoring mobility.</p></p>
