<p><strong>The Laboratory Chillers Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Water Cooled Laboratory Chillers</li><li>Air Cooled Laboratory Chillers</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The laboratory chillers market is primarily categorized into two types: water-cooled and air-cooled chillers. Water-cooled laboratory chillers utilize a water supply to dissipate heat, making them efficient for large-scale operations but requiring a water source and appropriate drainage. In contrast, air-cooled laboratory chillers use ambient air for cooling, making them easier to install and less dependent on external water resources. Both types serve critical roles in maintaining optimal temperatures for laboratory processes and equipment, enhancing operational efficiency.</p></p>
