<p><p>The Exterior Insulation and Finishing System (EIFS) market is segmented into several types based on material composition and application methods. Class Polymer Based (PB) EIFS utilizes a solely polymeric core for insulation and finish, offering excellent moisture resistance. Class Polymer Modified EIFS incorporates additives for enhanced flexibility and durability. Direct-Applied Exterior Finish System (DEFS) directly bonds the finish to the substrate without an insulation layer, providing a simpler application. Other markets may include niche products or hybrid systems designed for specific building needs.</p></p>
