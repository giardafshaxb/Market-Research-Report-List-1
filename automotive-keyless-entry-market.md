<p><strong>The Automotive Keyless Entry Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Passive Keyless Entry (PKE) System</li><li>Remote Keyless Entry (RKE) System</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The automotive keyless entry market primarily features two types: Passive Keyless Entry (PKE) systems and Remote Keyless Entry (RKE) systems. PKE systems allow drivers to unlock and start their vehicles without physically pressing buttons, relying on key fobs that communicate with the car when in proximity. In contrast, RKE systems require users to press buttons on a key fob to lock, unlock, or start the vehicle. Both systems enhance convenience and security, gaining popularity in modern vehicle designs.</p></p>
