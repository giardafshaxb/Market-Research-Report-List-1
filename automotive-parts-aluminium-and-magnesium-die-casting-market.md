<p><p>The automotive parts die casting market is divided into two main types: aluminium die casting and magnesium die casting. Aluminium die casting involves the use of lightweight, strong aluminium alloys, ideal for producing complex shapes with excellent surface finish and dimensional accuracy. This makes it popular for components like engine blocks and housings. Magnesium die casting, on the other hand, leverages the lighter weight of magnesium, enhancing fuel efficiency and reducing emissions, making it suitable for parts like transmission cases and structural components.</p></p>
