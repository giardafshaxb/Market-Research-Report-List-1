Peripheral Dual Energy X-Ray Absorptiometry (pDXA) offers portable, less invasive options for density measurements at peripheral sites like the wrist. Quantitative Ultrasound (QUS) employs sound waves to evaluate bone density without radiation exposure. Other devices encompass advanced imaging techniques and emerging technologies that enhance diagnostic capabilities.</p></p>
