<p><p>The bench top automatic cell sorter market includes several types catering to various sorting needs. Single cell sorters isolate individual cells for detailed analysis, while fluorescent activated cell sorters use fluorescence to separate cells based on specific markers. Magnetic cell sorters employ magnetic beads to facilitate the sorting of target cells, allowing for high specificity. Buoyancy activated cell sorters utilize density differences to sort cells based on their buoyancy, offering a unique approach to cell separation in research and clinical applications.</p></p>
