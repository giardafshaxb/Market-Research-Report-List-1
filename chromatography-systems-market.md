<p><strong>The Chromatography Systems Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>

<p><p>Chromatography systems are essential analytical techniques used to separate and analyze complex mixtures. Gas Chromatography (GC) utilizes vaporized samples for separation, ideal for volatile compounds. Liquid Chromatography (LC) employs liquid solvents to separate non-volatile substances. LC-MS (Liquid Chromatography-Mass Spectrometry) combines LC with mass spectrometry for enhanced detection and quantification of molecules. Similarly, GC-MS (Gas Chromatography-Mass Spectrometry) integrates GC with mass spectrometry, offering precise identification and analysis of gaseous samples. Together, these methods serve diverse applications in pharmaceuticals, environmental testing, and food safety.</p></p>
