<p><p>Pharmaceutical encapsulation technology encompasses microencapsulation and nanoencapsulation, both crucial for drug delivery systems. Microencapsulation involves enclosing active pharmaceutical ingredients within microscale coatings, enhancing stability and controlled release. This technique improves drug solubility and bioavailability. Nanoencapsulation, on the other hand, involves encapsulating drugs at the nanoscale, allowing for even smaller particle sizes, which can improve targeting and absorption at the cellular level. Both methods are vital for enhancing therapeutic efficacy and minimizing side effects in pharmaceuticals.</p></p>
