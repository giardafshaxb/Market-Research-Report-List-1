<p><strong>The Safety Capacitors Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Class-X Capacitors</li><li>Class-Y Capacitors</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Safety capacitors are designed to prevent electrical shock and ensure safe device operation. The Class-X capacitors are primarily used for line-to-line applications, protecting against surges and noise in power circuits. They are suitable for applications where the failure of the capacitor could result in electric shock. Class-Y capacitors, on the other hand, are used for line-to-ground connections, providing insulation and safety by reducing risk in fault conditions. Both types are essential in electronic and electrical equipment for compliance and safety standards.</p></p>
