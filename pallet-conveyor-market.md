<p><p>The pallet conveyor market encompasses various types designed for different operational needs. Drag chain conveyors are suited for heavy loads and tough environments, utilizing chains to move pallets. Roller conveyors come in driven or gravity styles, facilitating smooth movement either with motorized rollers or by gravity flow. Plastic type conveyors offer lightweight and corrosion-resistant options, ideal for food or pharmaceuticals. Slat type conveyors feature interlocking slats for stable transportation, commonly used in assembly lines and manufacturing environments.</p></p>
