<p><p>The connected vehicle device market comprises two primary types: built-in devices and retrofit devices. Built-in devices are integrated directly into the vehicle during manufacturing, offering seamless connectivity and optimized performance. In contrast, retrofit devices are aftermarket solutions that can be installed in existing vehicles, enhancing their connectivity features. Both types enable functionalities such as real-time data tracking, navigation, and vehicle diagnostics, contributing to improved safety, efficiency, and overall driving experience, while catering to different consumer needs and vehicle models.</p></p>
