<p><p>The polyester board market comprises three main types: Polyester MDF, Polyester Plywood, and Polyester Blockboard. Polyester MDF is made from wood fibers bonded with polyester resin, offering smooth surfaces ideal for intricate designs. Polyester Plywood combines layers of wood with a polyester finish, enhancing durability and moisture resistance. Polyester Blockboard consists of a core of solid wood strips sandwiched between layers of plywood and coated with polyester, delivering strength and stability, making it suitable for various structural applications.</p></p>
