<p><p>The preclinical imaging market encompasses various advanced imaging technologies used for research and development in drug discovery and disease understanding. Optical imaging systems utilize light to visualize biological processes, while nuclear imaging systems detect radioactive tracers. Micro-MRI systems offer high-resolution imaging for small animals, and micro-ultrasound systems provide detailed anatomical visualization. Micro-CT systems enable 3D imaging of internal structures, while preclinical photoacoustic imaging combines optical and ultrasound techniques. Lastly, preclinical magnetic particle imaging (MPI) allows for high-resolution imaging of magnetic nanoparticles in biological systems.</p></p>
