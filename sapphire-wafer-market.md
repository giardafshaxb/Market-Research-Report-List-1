<p><p>The sapphire wafer market encompasses various types, including A-Plane, C-Plane, and R-Plane wafers, each defined by their crystallographic orientation. A-Plane sapphire wafers feature crystal surfaces oriented parallel to the A-axis, making them suitable for specific optical and electronic applications. C-Plane wafers, with surfaces aligned to the C-axis, are commonly used in LEDs and electronic devices due to their favorable properties. R-Plane wafers, oriented at 45 degrees, are often used in specialized applications requiring unique polarization characteristics, enhancing performance across technologies.</p></p>
