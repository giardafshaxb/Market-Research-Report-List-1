<p><p>The frameless glass partition market primarily features two types: single glass partitions and double glass partitions. Single glass partitions utilize a single panel of tempered glass, providing sleek aesthetics and a minimalist look, ideal for smaller spaces. Conversely, double glass partitions consist of two panels with an air gap, enhancing sound insulation and thermal performance, making them suitable for offices and meeting rooms where privacy and acoustic control are important. Both options cater to modern architectural preferences while maximizing natural light.</p></p>
