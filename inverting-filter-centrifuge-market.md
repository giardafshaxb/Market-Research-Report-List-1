<p><p>The Inverting Filter Centrifuge market is categorized into two primary types: horizontal and vertical. Horizontal-type centrifuges feature a horizontal bowl orientation, allowing for continuous solids discharge and efficient separation of liquids and solids, making them ideal for large-scale processing. In contrast, vertical-type centrifuges have a vertical bowl configuration, which enhances solid-liquid separation in applications requiring higher centrifugal forces. Both types cater to various industries, ensuring effective separation and purification in processes involving slurries and suspensions.</p></p>
