<p><p>The spectrometer market can be categorized into atomic and molecular spectrometers. Atomic spectrometers analyze the elemental composition of samples by measuring light emitted or absorbed by atoms, often used in fields like chemistry and geology. In contrast, molecular spectrometers focus on analyzing chemical compounds by examining molecular vibrations and transitions, commonly used in environmental monitoring and pharmaceuticals. Both types serve essential roles in research and industry, facilitating detailed analysis of materials for quality control and scientific discovery.</p></p>
