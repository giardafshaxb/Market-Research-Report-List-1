<p><strong>The RNA Methylation Sequencing Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Radioisotope Incorporation</li><li>Thin-layer Chromatography</li><li>Mass Spectrometry</li><li>Differential Enzyme/Chemical-RNA Interactions</li><li>Bisulphite RNA Sequencing</li><li>Antibody-based Sequencing</li><li>Big Data</li></ul></p>
<p><p>RNA methylation sequencing includes various techniques to analyze RNA modifications. Radioisotope incorporation detects methylation through labeled substrates. Thin-layer chromatography separates RNA molecules by methylation status. Mass spectrometry offers precise identification and quantification of modified RNAs. Differential enzyme/chemical-RNA interactions reveal methylation patterns through enzymatic assays. Bisulphite RNA sequencing converts unmethylated cytosines, aiding in identification of modifications. Antibody-based sequencing utilizes specific antibodies to enrich modified RNAs for analysis. The big data market focuses on computational tools and storage for managing large-scale RNA methylation datasets.</p></p>
