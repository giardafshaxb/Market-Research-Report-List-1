<p><p>The CT High Pressure Injectors market comprises two main types: Single Head and Dual Head injectors. Single Head CT High Pressure Injectors are designed to deliver contrast agents for imaging procedures using a single injection site, providing efficiency and simplicity. In contrast, Dual Head CT High Pressure Injectors offer the capability to inject different contrast agents simultaneously or sequentially, enhancing imaging versatility and workflow in diagnostic settings. Both types play critical roles in improving patient care and diagnostic accuracy in computed tomography.</p></p>
