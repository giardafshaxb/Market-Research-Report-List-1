<p><strong>The Low Flow Magmeters Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Insertion Low Flow Meter</li><li>Inline Low Flow Meter</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Low Flow Magmeter market is divided into two primary types: insertion low flow meters and inline low flow meters. Insertion low flow meters are designed to be partially inserted into a pipe, allowing for measurement of fluid flow without extensive modifications. They are suitable for varying pipe sizes. Inline low flow meters, on the other hand, are integrated directly into the piping system and provide continuous, precise flow measurements, making them ideal for applications requiring high accuracy and reliability.</p></p>
