<p><p>Microfluidic chip processing equipment encompasses various technologies for fabricating intricate microstructures. Photolithography equipment uses light to transfer patterns onto substrates. Laser processing equipment employs focused laser beams for precision cutting and structuring. Micromachining equipment utilizes mechanical processes to create micro-scale features. Hot pressing equipment combines heat and pressure to mold materials, while injection molding equipment forms chips by injecting polymers into molds. Plasma processing equipment utilizes plasma to treat surfaces and etch patterns, with other methods offering alternative fabrication techniques.</p></p>
