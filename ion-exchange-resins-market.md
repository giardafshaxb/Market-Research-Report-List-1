<p><strong>The Ion Exchange Resins Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Anion Exchange Resin</li><li>Cation Exchange Resin</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The ion exchange resins market is primarily divided into two types: anion exchange resins and cation exchange resins. Anion exchange resins are used to remove negatively charged ions, often employed in water purification and deionization processes. Cation exchange resins, on the other hand, target positively charged ions, playing a crucial role in softening hard water and enhancing chemical processes. Both types are essential in various industries, including water treatment, pharmaceuticals, and food processing, facilitating efficient ion removal and exchange.</p></p>
